# AFOS Daily · Daily Synthesis

> Wednesday, May 13 reverses the presidential board in an unprecedented movement in the recent series. [CNN Brasil made available in full the audio in which Flávio Bolsonaro negotiates R$ 134 million with Daniel Vorcaro](https://www.cnnbrasil.com.br/politica/ouca-o-audio-de-flavio-bolsonaro-pedindo-dinheiro-a-vorcaro/) for the film "Dark Horse" about Jair Bolsonaro (original reporting from Intercept Brasil), and [Polymarket](/en/glossary#polymarket) moves 21,90pp in 24h: [Lula SURGES to 45,50% (↑7,00pp) and Flávio PLUMMETS to 28,45% (↓14,90pp)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election), reversing the gap from +4,85pp Flávio on May 12 to +17,05pp Lula. The national [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) published on May 13 marked Lula 39% × Flávio 33% in the [1º turno](/en/glossary#primeiro-turno) and [Lula 42% × Flávio 41% in the 2º turno (Genial/Quaest, JOTA)](https://www.jota.info/eleicoes/eleicoes-2026/lula-reassume-vantagem-numerica-sobre-flavio-bolsonaro-no-2o-turno-diz-quaest).

## 1. Prediction market

On [Polymarket](/en/glossary#polymarket) at 19:00 BRT, **Lula SURGES to 45,50% (↑7,00pp from 38,50%)** — the largest individual movement in 24h of the recent series in the presidential market. **Flávio Bolsonaro PLUMMETS to 28,45% (↓14,90pp from 43,35%)** — the largest drop in 24h of the series. The gap reverses completely: it was +4,85pp Flávio on May 12 and closed at +17,05pp Lula on May 13, a variation of 21,90pp between two daily snapshots. The movement occurs on the same day [CNN Brasil published the audio in which Flávio negotiates R$ 134 million with Daniel Vorcaro](https://www.cnnbrasil.com.br/politica/ouca-o-audio-de-flavio-bolsonaro-pedindo-dinheiro-a-vorcaro/) to finance the film "Dark Horse".

In the third way, [**Romeu Zema SURGES presidentially to 12,00% (↑8,15pp from 3,85%)**](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election) — the largest individual jump in 24h of the series in the third-way range. **Renan Santos SURGES to 7,05% (↑1,20pp)**. The two absorb part of the migration leaving Flávio. **Michelle Bolsonaro SURGES to 1,95% (↑1,50pp)** in a scenario where [VEJA reported that Bolsonarists debate in groups a possible replacement of Flávio](https://veja.abril.com.br/politica/dark-horse-o-que-e-o-filme-que-virou-pivo-da-maior-crise-da-campanha-de-flavio-bolsonaro/) — early signal attributed by the outlet, not a consolidated decision.

In [**2nd place**](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-2nd-place), Flávio yields to 61,00% (↓6,50pp from 67,50%) but maintains absolute leadership of the position. Lula 15,50% (↓1,00pp). Zema rises to 7,25% (↑4,30pp). In [**3rd place**](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-3rd-place), Zema recovers leadership of the dispute with 35,00% (↑2,50pp), followed by Renan at 31,50% and Caiado stable at 18,50%.

The [**STF Impeach COLLAPSES to 6,50% (↓4,00pp from 10,50%)**](https://polymarket.com/event/any-brazil-stf-justice-removed-by-impeachment-before-2027) — the downward trajectory accumulates 9,00pp in 48h and appears to reflect that the direct electoral crisis absorbs attention that previously orbited the anti-STF narrative.

In the **Senate**, the [PL](/en/glossary#pl) recovers to 77,00% (↑2,00pp from 75,00%) even with Flávio's individual crisis. The [MDB](/en/glossary#mdb) returns strong to 5,00% (↓3,65pp from 8,65%), Podemos returns to 1,35% (↓1,70pp). [PT](/en/glossary#pt) consolidates at 3,05% (↑0,15pp). In **2026 inflation**, the 4,50-4,99% band SURGES to 18,95% (↑6,75pp), and the high bands (≥6,50%) RETURN colossally to 17,20% (↓23,10pp from 40,30% on May 12), reversing the extreme fiscal narrative priced in the day before.

## 2. What the institutes registered

The **Quaest/Genial national**, field from May 8 to 11 and sample n=2.004, published on May 13 and marked **Lula 39%, Flávio Bolsonaro 33%, Caiado 4% and Zema 4%** in the [1º turno](/en/glossary#primeiro-turno) (according to G1, Gazeta do Povo and CartaCapital). In the **[2º turno](/en/glossary#segundo-turno)**, [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) registered Lula 42% × Flávio 41% (Genial/Quaest, JOTA) — [Empate técnico](/en/glossary#empate-tecnico), but with Lula numerically ahead for the first time in the institute's recent series.

[Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) itself also registered **improvement in Lula's evaluation, growth in voting intention and fall in rejection** (according to Jornal Opção and Brasil 247). Estadão observed in analysis that voters see neither Lula nor Flávio Bolsonaro as moderate names, signaling that Lula's recovery on [Polymarket](/en/glossary#polymarket) is not only about the crisis on the right, but about voter composition in which polarization is not seen as a positive attribute. The next national publication is [Datafolha](/en/glossary#datafolha) n=2.004 on May 15 (D+1 of AFOS launch), covering Trump, [STF](/en/glossary#stf) and Desenrola, and should serve as an institutional validation test for [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) numbers.

State surveys published on May 13 concentrate on regional maps. The [TSE](/en/glossary#tse) registered on May 13 eight state surveys with publication scheduled for May 15-17 ([Datafolha](/en/glossary#datafolha) n=2.004 national + Veritá n=3.330, Doxa, Qualitta, ECM, Falabella, Parla Mentors, Perfil — all state-level).## 3. The three signals (leak + admission + research) converge on the same day and the market prices deterioration even more pronounced than research records.

2. **The third way reorganizes with Zema absorbing more space than Caiado on [Polymarket](/en/glossary#polymarket)**, despite [empate técnico](/en/glossary#empate-tecnico) in [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) [1º turno](/en/glossary#primeiro-turno) (4% × 4%). Renan also advances (↑1.20pp), and Michelle Bolsonaro SURGES 1.95% (↑1.50pp) in a scenario where Flávio's replacement is a topic discussed in Bolsonarist groups according to VEJA.

3. **The [STF](/en/glossary#stf) Impeach continues collapsing** (from 10.50% to 6.50%, accumulating 9.00pp decline in 48h) and the high bands of inflation return 23.10pp of the expansion recorded on 05/12. The market appears to be reorganizing anti-establishment and extreme fiscal narratives while the direct electoral crisis absorbs main attention — [Datafolha](/en/glossary#datafolha) on 05/15 will be the next test of institutional validation of the repositioning.—
