## 1. Prediction market

On [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election) from May 10 at 22:00 BRT, Lula jumps to 40.50% (↑3.00pp in 24 hours) — the largest individual move of the recent series in the presidential race — while **Flávio Bolsonaro** retreats to 41.60% (↓2.05pp). The result is a **compression of the gap by 5.05pp in a single window** (+1.10pp Flávio now, versus +6.15pp on May 9). The move occurs without a single attributable catalyst: May 10 is Sunday, Mother's Day, with no new national polls published. The predominant technical reading is digestion of the previous week's agenda (Moraes' decision on Lei da Dosimetria, Lula × Trump meeting, Operation Compliance Zero against Ciro Nogueira) combined with **anticipation of the national [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest)**, [whose results are scheduled for May 13](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMixwFBVV95cUxON0RmV1pfbmdNcjI0TS1oaFdLQ1JzNW5LY3Z2eHItUDZqRjU1MEtjN19mR3Z5SmRSMEpzM25jRjJkZGpvS3pXMzRCS2w2aC1jNV8xZ3V5OFlTTnpCQkRVZHdLMnhIOGVQLTV2cUh4SzZjM2d1YlprMWtJQ05KZm16YWhLNFpjTjNDWjFhaDlJejRmLTViWUpWU3NGVTJNbnZMM1dsT2tHRHEzVC04Z3prbUxBLV9GQUFWU240dXJ6WEdVa2RnZzhn?oc=5).

In the contest for [second place in the first round](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-2nd-place), Flávio remains at 66.50% (stable), and Lula at 19.50% (stable). Alckmin rises to 1.90% (↑0.30pp) and Haddad recovers to 3.05% (↑0.10pp), reflecting modest consolidation of the government alliance base in second place.

In the race for [third place in the first round](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-3rd-place), Zema maintains leadership at 34.50% (↓0.50pp slight), Renan Santos falls to 31.50% (↓1.00pp), and **Caiado surges to 16.50% (↑3.00pp)**, recovering ground ceded on May 9. This is the largest individual jump in the third-place race in 24 hours. Flávio retreats to 4.80% and Haddad to 3.90%.

The sharpest movement outside the presidential race is in [STF impeachment, advancing 2.50pp to 16.50%](https://polymarket.com/event/any-brazil-stf-justice-removed-by-impeachment-before-2027), the highest value of the recent series. The move is consistent with prolonged repercussion of Moraes' monocratic decision suspending Lei da Dosimetria on May 9, combined with news that [Kassio Nunes Marques takes office as president of TSE](https://redir.folha.com.br/redir/online/poder/rss091/*https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/poder/2026/05/kassio-assumira-presidencia-do-tse-para-comandar-eleicoes-de-2026-veja-historico-da-corte.shtml) on Tuesday (12) seeking a profile distinct from Moraes — signaling to the market an internal split in the Court.

In the [Senate](https://polymarket.com/event/next-brazil-senate-election-most-seats-won), total reorganization of probabilities in 24 hours: [PL](/en/glossary#pl) surges to 83.00% (↑1.50pp), **Podemos rises colossally to 7.95% (↑6.55pp, from 1.40%)**, **[PSB](/en/glossary#psb) rises strongly to 4.80% (↑3.55pp)**, and [Republicanos](/en/glossary#republicanos) jump to 3.85% (↑2.85pp), while **[PT](/en/glossary#pt) retreats colossally to 0.25% (↓2.30pp)** and [PSD](/en/glossary#psd) retreats strongly to 2.25% (↓2.00pp). The move has no direct news catalyst and appears to reflect repricing of coalition scenarios. On [2026 inflation](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-annual-inflation-2026), the 5.00–5.49% band surges to 34.95% (↑2.20pp) and maintains leadership; high bands (≥6.50%) retreat slightly to 26.50% (↓1.10pp).

## 4. Divergences of the day

> **Market × event:** The presidential gap fell 5.05pp in 24 hours on [Polymarket](/en/glossary#polymarket), but May 10 (Sunday, Mother's Day) had no clear triggering factual event. The defensible reading is digestion of the previous week's agenda and anticipation of the [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) poll on the 13th. Be careful not to attribute the movement to a single factor.

> **Market × poll:** Real Time Big Data from May 5 showed Lula 40% × Flávio 34% (gap +6pp). [AtlasIntel](/en/glossary#atlasintel) from April 28 showed Lula 46.6% × Flávio 39.7% (gap +6.9pp). [Polymarket](/en/glossary#polymarket) today (gap +1.10pp Flávio) shows visible disagreement with the two most recent TSE references. The [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) poll on May 13 will be the test of which reading prevails.

> **Senate market × absence of catalyst:** [Podemos](/en/glossary#podemos) rises colossally 6.55pp and [PSB](/en/glossary#psb) rises 3.55pp with no attributable event. [PT](/en/glossary#pt) falls colossally 2.30pp. The movement suggests repricing of coalition scenarios, but the magnitude calls for caution — it may also reflect low liquidity on the weekend.

## In summary

1. **The collapse of the gap is the most relevant signal of the window.** Lula recovers ground ceded between May 1 and 9, and the spread returns to the level of [Empate técnico](/en/glossary#empate-tecnico) priced by national polls (Real Time, [AtlasIntel](/en/glossary#atlasintel)). The [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) poll on [May 13](https://veja.abril.com.br/politica/nova-pesquisa-quaest-mostra-disputa-lula-x-flavio-a-menos-de-cinco-meses-da-eleicao/) is the expected catalyst to confirm or refute this convergence.
