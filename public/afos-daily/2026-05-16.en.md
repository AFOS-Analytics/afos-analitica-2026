## 1. Prediction market

On the [Polymarket presidential](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election), Lula remains stable at **44.50%** (no change in 36 hours) while Flávio Bolsonaro recovers to **32.50%** (↑4.15pp in 24h, ↑3.65pp in 36h vs 28.85% on May 15 morning). The gap narrows to **+12.00pp Lula** (vs +15.65pp on May 15 morning, ↓3.65pp in 36h). The broad lead priced in after the Flávio–Vorcaro audio is partially returned with the [Datafolha published in the first round (Folha de S.Paulo)](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/poder/2026/05/pesquisa-mostra-que-flavio-estancou-antes-de-dark-horse-dizem-aliados-de-lula-oposicao-minimiza.shtml) signaling a tighter race than the market had been pricing.

In the third way, Renan Santos rises to **9.75%** (↑0.40pp in 24h) and surpasses Romeu Zema by **5.65%** (↓1.20pp in 24h) for the first time at D+2 of launch. Caiado continues declining in the presidential at 1.15% (↓0.40pp in 36h), but recovers to third place at **18.50%** (↑2.00pp in 24h). Michelle Bolsonaro holds 2.80% stable; the narrative of [PL](/en/glossary#pl) ticket substitution loses traction at 2L (↓0.45pp in 24h to 2.85%).

On the [runoff Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-2nd-place), Flávio maintains absolute lead at **60.50%** (↓2.50pp in 24h, light decline without rupture) × Lula 15.00% stable. On the [third place Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-3rd-place), Zema cedes the top at **32.50%** (↓7.00pp in 24h) while Renan firms up just below at 31.00% stable.

The pricing of [impeachment STF before 2027](https://polymarket.com/event/any-brazil-stf-justice-removed-by-impeachment-before-2027) reverts yesterday's reversal collapse: falls to **7.25%** (↓1.75pp in 24h, ↓5.50pp in 36h) after the peak of 12.75% on May 15 and returns to the 8% level registered at close of May 14 evening. The market prices in that individual processes via [PF](/en/glossary#pf)/common courts plus the possibility of Master CPI in the Legislature absorb the investigative axis of the [STF](/en/glossary#stf) theme.

On [Senate 2026 most seats](https://polymarket.com/event/next-brazil-senate-election-most-seats-won), [PL](/en/glossary#pl) retreats to 78.00% (↓2.00pp in 24h) while [MDB](/en/glossary#mdb) surges to **16.55%** (↑14.70pp in 24h) — atypical movement in low-volume market (USD 254 thousand), which warrants caution in interpretation. [Republicanos](/en/glossary#republicanos) yields to 10.40% (↓0.75pp in 24h, ↓1.25pp in 36h vs 11.65% on May 15). On [annual inflation 2026](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-annual-inflation-2026), the central band 5.00-5.49% yields to 26.05% (↓4.80pp in 24h) with redistribution to neighboring bands; the grouping of high bands (≥6.50%) consolidates at 13.20% (↓2.70pp in 24h).
