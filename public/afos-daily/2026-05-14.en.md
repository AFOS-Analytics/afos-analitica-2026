# May 14, 2026

> Thursday (May 14) begins with [the Federal Police arresting Henrique Vorcaro, father of Daniel Vorcaro, in the 6th phase of Operation Compliance Zero](https://oglobo.globo.com/blogs/miriam-leitao/post/2026/05/pai-de-daniel-vorcaro-e-preso.ghtml). The prediction market partially digests the shock from the previous day — Lula dips slightly to 42.50% (↓3.00pp), Flávio recovers to 33.35% (↑4.90pp), and the gap between the two narrows from +17.05pp to +9.15pp in Lula's favor. [STF](/en/glossary#stf) impeach continues collapsing (4.50%, ↓2.00pp), accumulating 13.50pp in 72h.

## 1. Prediction market

On [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election) at 09:00 BRT, **Lula dips slightly to 42.50% (↓3.00pp in 24h)** and **Flávio Bolsonaro recovers to 33.35% (↑4.90pp)**. The gap between the two narrows from +17.05pp Lula on May 13 to **+9.15pp Lula on May 14** — Lula maintains the lead but the margin is cut in half in 24 hours. The movement is consistent with market digestion: the Lula spike on May 13 (following the Flávio-Vorcaro audio) begins to recalibrate as the news settles and the [PL](/en/glossary#pl) base resists.

The **third way has two relevant movements in opposite directions**. [Romeu Zema (Novo) dips sharply to 5.85% (↓6.15pp from 12.00%)](/en/glossary#novo) — the spike on May 13 does not hold. [Renan Santos (Missão/MBL) remains stable at 7.15% (↑0.10pp slight)](/en/glossary#mdb). Renan now surpasses Zema in the presidential race for the first time in the series, although both remain nominally tied in the [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) poll on May 13 at 4% each. [Caiado (PSD) rises slightly to 1.35% (↑0.10pp)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election). [Haddad yields to 2.45% (↓0.20pp)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election).

In the **second place market**, [Flávio recovers to 66.00% (↑5.00pp from 61.00%)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-2nd-place) solidifying absolute leadership. But the highlight is **Michelle Bolsonaro, who continues rising in the presidential race to 3.40% (↑1.45pp) and surges in second place to 3.60% (↑2.60pp from 1.00%)** — the market prices the replacement narrative with growing strength, three days after [VEJA reported that Bolsonaristas discuss replacing her with Flávio](https://veja.abril.com.br/politica/em-grupo-bolsonaristas-debatem-substituicao-de-flavio-por-michelle-apos-audio-pedindo-recursos-para-vorcaro/). [Lula in second place rises slightly to 16.00% (↑0.50pp)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-2nd-place).

The **third place market** reorganizes significantly. [Zema maintains top position at 35.50% (↑0.50pp stable)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-3rd-place), [Renan surges to 34.50% (↑3.00pp)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-3rd-place) and [Caiado dips sharply to 13.00% (↓5.50pp from 18.50%)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-3rd-place). Renan and Zema are now technically tied for the first time in the race for third place.

The **[STF](/en/glossary#stf) minister impeachment market** [continues collapsing to 4.50% (↓2.00pp from 6.50%)](https://polymarket.com/event/any-brazil-stf-justice-removed-by-impeachment-before-2027) — accumulated drop of 13.50pp in 72 hours. The market prices that investigations via [PF](/en/glossary#pf)/PGR (Operation Compliance Zero against the Master case) replace the impeachment path via politics. In the [Senate, PL consolidates 79.50% (↑2.50pp)](https://polymarket.com/event/next-brazil-senate-election-most-seats-won), [PSD surges to 3.05% (↑1.00pp from 2.05%)](https://polymarket.com/event/next-brazil-senate-election-most-seats-won) and [Republicanos dips sharply to 1.05% (↓4.20pp from 5.25%)](https://polymarket.com/event/next-brazil-senate-election-most-seats-won) — the market abandons [Republicanos](/en/glossary#republicanos) as a hypothesis for protagonist strength. In [2026 inflation](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-annual-inflation-2026), the high bands (≥6.50%) partially recover to 20.35% (↑3.15pp), with the 7.00%+ interval surging to 10.40% (↑2.20pp).

The reading is one of **market digestion**: the spike against Flávio on May 13 (following the audio) recalibrates as the [PL](/en/glossary#pl) base holds firm and the news cycle settles. It is not direct causation from any specific May 14 event — it is technical correction after an unprecedented 24-hour move the previous day.

> **Zema × press:** [Polymarket](/en/glossary#polymarket) returns Zema from 12.00% to 5.85% (↓6.15pp) — the May 13 spike does not confirm. The May 14 press does not cite Zema among the day's movements; the market prices in that he did not consolidate as the recipient of the migration expected from the Flávio crisis. At the same time, [Renan surges into 3rd place at 34.50% (↑3.00pp)](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-3rd-place) without appearing prominently in [Quaest](/en/glossary#quaest) May 13 or in today's coverage — the market reads an internal reorganization of the third way that has not yet been mirrored in coverage.

> **[STF](/en/glossary#stf) impeachment × institutional dynamics:** the market for impeachment of an [STF](/en/glossary#stf) minister collapses to 4.50% (↓2.00pp in 24 hours, ↓13.50pp in 72 hours), while today's press highlights [PF](/en/glossary#pf) advancing on **three parallel investigative fronts** (arrest of Henrique Vorcaro, groups "A Turma" and "Os Meninos", concealment of R$ 2.2 billion via his father's account). The reading is coherent: the market prices in that individual proceedings via [PF](/en/glossary#pf)/common courts replace the impeachment path via politics, especially after Fachin tightened rules for case distribution at the [STF](/en/glossary#stf) (May 12) and Kassio's swearing-in at the [TSE](/en/glossary#tse) was completed.
