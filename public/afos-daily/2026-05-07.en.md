## 1. Prediction market

There was no specific triggering event on May 7 for this movement, and the data is consistent with a technical reading of the exit from the compression peak observed in the four previous sessions. In the dispute for [2nd place](https://polymarket.com/event/brazil-presidential-election-first-round-2nd-place), **Flávio consolidated at 65.50%** (↑0.50pp from 65.00%), Lula remained at **19.50%** (stable), Camilo Santana recovered to **4.85%** (↑1.00pp from 3.85%), Haddad stable at **3.55%** and Zema stable at **3.35%**.

In the presidential race, **Zema jumped to 3.95%** (↑0.40pp from 3.55%, recovering from May 6's drop), **Caiado jumped to 1.15%** (↑0.20pp from 0.95%, back above the 1% level), **Renan stable at 5.85%**, **Haddad gave up slightly to 3.35%** (↓0.10pp from 3.45%) and **Camilo devolved sharply to 2.25%** (↓1.30pp from 3.55%) — Haddad is now above Camilo, reversing the fragmentation observed in previous days.

The movement occurred on a day that saw a new [PF](/en/glossary#pf) operation approved by minister André Mendonça ([STF](/en/glossary#stf)) against Senator Ciro Nogueira, [according to Imirante reporting](https://imirante.com/noticias/brasil/2026/05/07/ipolitica-ciro-nogueira-e-alvo-da-5a-fase-da-operacao-compliance-zero), and [Folha reporting on the political interpretation of the operation](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/colunas/painel/2026/05/operacao-contra-ciro-nogueira-abala-eleicao-e-e-aperitivo-de-delacao-de-vorcaro.shtml). ## 3. What the press covered

**The political agenda on May 7 was dominated by the meeting between Lula and Trump at the White House**, which lasted three hours behind closed doors. According to **[InfoMoney reporting](https://www.infomoney.com.br/politica/lula-deixa-casa-branca-apos-reuniao-de-tres-horas-com-trump-a-portas-fechadas/)**, Trump called the meeting "very good" and said representatives from both countries should meet in the future to address "key points". Topics on the agenda included trade, tariffs, critical minerals and combating organized crime, according to **[Poder360](https://www.poder360.com.br/poder-governo/lula-e-trump-se-reunem-para-discutir-economia-e-seguranca/)**. **[Folha reported that the Planalto was betting on the Trump meeting to isolate Flávio Bolsonaro and reinforce Lula's institutional role](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/poder/2026/05/planalto-aposta-em-reuniao-com-trump-para-tentar-isolar-flavio-e-reforcar-papel-de-lula.shtml)** and that **[Lula was accompanied by five ministers and the [PF](/en/glossary#pf) director on the visit](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/blogs/brasilia-hoje/2026/05/lula-levara-quatro-ministros-e-diretor-da-pf-em-visita-a-trump.shtml)**.

In the judicial-political sphere, **the [PF](/en/glossary#pf) launched on May 7 the 5th phase of Operation Compliance Zero against Senator Ciro Nogueira (PP-PI)**, former Civil House minister in the Bolsonaro government. According to **[Imirante](https://imirante.com/noticias/brasil/2026/05/07/ipolitica-ciro-nogueira-e-alvo-da-5a-fase-da-operacao-compliance-zero)** and **[Diário Carioca](https://diariocarioca.com/2026/05/07/politica/ciro-nogueira-banco-master-operacao/)**, one temporary arrest warrant and ten search and seizure warrants authorized by minister André Mendonça ([STF](/en/glossary#stf)) were served, with R$ 18.85 million blocked. The operation investigates alleged undue transfers linked to banker Daniel Vorcaro and [Banco Master](/en/glossary#banco-master). According to **[Folha's Panel column reporting](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/colunas/painel/2026/05/operacao-contra-ciro-nogueira-abala-eleicao-e-e-aperitivo-de-delacao-de-vorcaro.shtml)**, the operation "shakes the election" and anticipates the content of Vorcaro's plea deal. **[Lula declared he hopes all those under investigation are innocent](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/poder/2026/05/espero-que-todos-os-investigados-sejam-inocentes-diz-lula-sobre-operacao-que-atingiu-ciro-nogueira.shtml)** (Folha May 7), and **[Flávio Bolsonaro released a statement calling the operation news "serious" without mentioning Ciro Nogueira](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/poder/2026/05/flavio-bolsonaro-divulga-nota-sem-citar-ciro-nogueira-e-diz-que-noticias-sobre-operacao-sao-graves.shtml)**. **[The Panel column also reported that the action leads Flávio's campaign to reconsider the support of PP and União Brasil](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/colunas/painel/2026/05/acao-contra-ciro-coloca-em-duvida-apoio-de-pp-e-uniao-a-flavio.shtml)**. According to **[Brasil 247](https://www.brasil247.com/brasil/planalto-orienta-ministros-a-evitar-comentarios-sobre-acao-da-pf-contra-ciro-nogueira)**, the Planalto instructed ministers not to comment on the operation.

In institutional developments with the Senate, **[O Globo, in a column by Malu Gaspar, reported that Davi Alcolumbre allegedly asked Lula for protection against possible plea deal by Daniel Vorcaro two weeks before the rejection of Jorge Messias to the [STF](/en/glossary#stf), with the president responding that he does not control [PF](/en/glossary#pf), AGU and [STF](/en/glossary#stf)](https://oglobo.globo.com/blogs/malu-gaspar/post/2026/05/antes-de-derrotar-messias-alcolumbre-pediu-a-lula-blindagem-contra-delacao-de-vorcaro.ghtml)** — the reporting notes that Alcolumbre's office denies the conversation in a statement to O Globo. **[O Globo also reported that [PF](/en/glossary#pf) director-general Andrei Rodrigues called Alcolumbre to warn him about the operation against Ciro Nogueira](https://oglobo.globo.com/politica/noticia/2026/05/07/andrei-rodrigues-ligou-para-alcolumbre-para-avisar-sobre-operacao-da-pf-que-mirou-ciro-nogueira.ghtml)** and that **[the government calls in "firefighters" to rebuild bridge with Alcolumbre after Messias's defeat](https://oglobo.globo.com/politica/noticia/2026/05/07/governo-aciona-bombeiros-para-reconstruir-ponte-com-alcolumbre-apos-derrota-de-messias.ghtml)**. **[Folha reported in parallel that Alcolumbre himself asked for a meeting with Lula to restore relations](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/poder/2026/05/alcolumbre-pede-agenda-com-lula-para-restabelecer-relacao-apos-derrota-de-messias.shtml)** and that **[Lula ordered to reestablish relations and the government approached Alcolumbre](https://www1.folha.uol.com.br/poder/2026/05/lula-manda-reatar-relacao-e-governo-procura-alcolumbre-apos-derrota-de-messias.shtml)** after the defeat.
